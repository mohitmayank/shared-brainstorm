Your AI coding agent plans alone — but the real product decisions need your team.

shared-brainstorm pulls your teammates into your agent's planning loop: your agent asks a design question ("Postgres or Redis?"), your team weighs in on a live web page, and your agent keeps planning with their answer. Nothing to install for teammates, no sign-ups, no accounts — just a link they open in any browser.

Works with Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, and Gemini CLI.

▶ Get started in one command:
   npx shared-brainstorm --install claude-code

What you get:
• Link-only join — you approve each teammate (no join codes, no accounts)
• Live suggestions, comments, and clarifying questions back to the AI
• Batch questions answered in any order
• A coordinator web UI to drive the session — or run it all from your terminal
• Add your own answer to the pool, then pick the final from every candidate
• A transcript of every decision saved to disk
